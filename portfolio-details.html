Detection of Safety Equipment Violation

Introduction

These demo videos show that there are kinds of safety violation are detected, including not wearing helmet, not wearing safety vest and being topless. After integrating it into the monitor system, the construction manager can supervise the unsafe behavior of workers immediately.

This project is also my master thesis. By combining object detection model and classification model, and training them with 3000 images collected form site, the result can achieve over 0.9 precision and recall in our experiment.

To get more detailed information, please visit National Digital Library of Theses and Dissertations.
